Other Services & Products

Captive Insurance

Other than paying premiums to third-party insurers, we provide an alternative solution for you to insure your own risks by establishing a captive insurance company which is wholly owned and controlled by yourself. While taking more financial control and managing risks by underwriting your own insurance, you may also benefit from the captive insurer's underwriting profits. With experts who are experienced in captive management, we can tailor-make the coverage that meets your need and help improve the cash flow.

Risk Consulting

Risk can be anywhere also difficult to detect. Our Risk Consulting team helps you identify, understand, and manage risks related to your business. Our experts will conduct Risk Analytics for you in order to discover the potential risks and provide solutions for risk control. We provide a wide range of risk consulting services to help your business manage, prevent, and reduce risks, whatever your industry.

Claims Management

Efficient claim management is vital if you would like to minimize your possible loss. With our expertise and experience, we provide bespoke advice for you to promptly response to the claim. We help you identify the facts of a claim, conduct an analysis of the potential liability and recommend how the claim could be covered under your insurance policy. We also provide suggestions on how to undertake actions to minimize the impact in every aspect and improve the claims management strategies.
