Solomon Chiu

Chairman

Our Founder

Solomon has more than 30 years of experience in the risk management and insurance industry. His industry experience includes senior leadership roles at Evergreen Group, where he was also a Board Director of the Evergreen Insurance Company, Bermuda, responsible for all management and operations, and President of Central Re.
Previously, Solomon was a senior management of ACR. He led ACR’s rapid expansion in Greater China during a 10-year span in which it became one of the largest reinsurers in Asia, negotiating and completing many reinsurance businesses.
He has twice won Risk Manager of the Year 1994(Singapore) and 2002(Tokyo), Chairman of Risk Management Society of Taiwan, and Licenced Master Mariner of Ocean Going ships. Solomon earned his EMBA from National Chengchi University.
